RULES ON THE USE OF MCRAC UNIFORM AND OTHER ORGANIZATION ITEMS

MCRAC Daily Uniform

A black shirt with red or black collar and with the logo patches of MCRAC and FIPO is the official uniform of MCRAC partnered with MCRAC tactical pants.

Black With Red Collar MCRAC Daily Uniform

The MCRAC daily uniforms can be worn anytime, anywhere especially if representing the organization in community and government meetings or offices. Wearing MCRAC uniform inside casinos, bars, parties or any other places where liquor drinks are present is strictly prohibited. It is also strictly prohibited if used to abuse or to intimidate other people especially traffic law enforcers or any authorities. Such actions will be dealt with accordingly.

MCRAC Vest

A black vest with the logo patches of MCRAC and FIPO. This vest can be worn anytime, anywhere except inside casinos, bars, parties or any other places where liquor drinks are present. It is also strictly prohibited if used to abuse, bully or intimidate other people especially traffic law enforcers or any authorities. Such actions will be dealt with accordingly.

MCRAC Task Force Disiplina Traffic Vest

A neon-green reflectorized vest used by Task Force Disiplina volunteers. This vest has MCRAC TFD patches and used with the corresponding TFD ID to help assist and ease traffic in the metro. NOT A PASS and definitely NOT TO BE USED to intimidate any law enforcers to get away with traffic violations but merely to serve the motorists and the public. Unlawful use and abuse will be dealt with accordingly.

MCRAC Task Force Disiplina Helmet

This helmet is part of the Task Force Disiplina Uniform. A must for motorcycle TFD volunteer to help protect the rider in case of accidents and to follow the mandatory law of Motorcycle Helmet Act of 2009 or the RA 10054.

MCRAC Firing Shirt

This shirt is used whenever MCRAC organizes a fun shoot. This serves as the official firing shirt of the organization.

MCRAC Badges (Badge Wallet And Belt Badge)

The MCRAC badge is purely for identification only and NOT A PASS when a member violates any kind of law. It is not used to abuse or bully other people or to intimidate authorities. Unauthorized use and abuse will be dealt with accordingly and would mean termination from the organization.

MMCRAC Dog Tag

The MCRAC dog tag is also for identification only and as a pure sign of brotherhood. By wearing this, other members will be able to know and recognize the wearer that he is an MCRAC brother. NOT A PASS against any violations.

MCRAC Bull Cap

Part of MCRAC Uniform that helps minimize sun’s heat rays during the day. Can be worn at all times only by legit members.

MMCRAC Motor Plate

Simply used for the promotional aspect of the organization. NOT A PASS against violations of any kind. Not to be placed on top or as an exchange of an LTO plate. Apprehended violators will not be tolerated. Proper positioning is either on top or below the LTO motor plate
